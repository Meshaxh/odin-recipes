Best-Chicken Salad Recipe

How to Make Chicken Salad

This homemade chicken salad recipe uses six simple ingredients and takes only 10 minutes to make from start to finish. After that, you can serve it up on salad greens, scoop it into pita bread or half an avocado, roll it up in a tortilla for a wrap, or serve it hot in a quesadilla or chicken melt.

Chicken Salad Ingredients

These are the simple ingredients you'll need to make this classic chicken salad recipe:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or chopped (rotisserie or poached chicken works well)
  • ½ cup mayonnaise (regular or reduced-fat)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black peppe
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup red onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or pecans, optional)
  • ¼ cup grapes or dried cranberries, halved (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Make the dressing:

    In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, black pepper, and a pinch of salt. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed

  2. Combine ingredients:

    In a large mixing bowl, add chicken, celery, onion, nuts, and fruit (if using). Pour the dressing over and stir until well combined.

  3. Chill (optional):

    Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let flavors meld.

  4. Serve:

    Enjoy on sandwiches, wraps, with crackers, or over a bed of greens
